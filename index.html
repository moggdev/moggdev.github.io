FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you buy, hold, or build with MOGG.

What is MOGG ($MOGG)?
MOGG ($MOGG) is a meme coin on the Solana blockchain built around the cultural concept of "mogging" — effortlessly outclassing the competition. It combines dark luxury aesthetics with a trust-first token structure: fixed supply, zero tax, and permanently revoked authorities.
What does "mint revoked" and "freeze revoked" mean?
On Solana, a token creator can hold special authorities over the token contract. Mint authority allows them to create new tokens, inflating supply. Freeze authority allows them to lock any wallet holding the token. By revoking both, MOGG's team has permanently surrendered these privileges. No new tokens can ever be minted, and no holder's wallet can ever be frozen. This is fully verifiable on-chain via Solana Explorer.
Why is the tax 0%?
A zero-tax structure means every swap is executed at face value — no hidden fees siphoned from buy or sell transactions. This improves price efficiency, reduces friction for traders, and removes a common vector for malicious rug mechanics. What you swap is exactly what you receive.
Where can I buy MOGG?
MOGG is currently available on Solana DEXs including Raydium and Jupiter. The easiest entry point is via Phantom's built-in swap. Always verify the contract address 5ZifNSEV5S6DHGUKC7wxzruNur5HLLMBECWqDsTuWen7 before executing any transaction.
What wallet should I use?
Phantom Wallet is recommended for Solana-based tokens. It's available as a browser extension and mobile app, supports in-app swapping, and provides a clean interface for managing SPL tokens like MOGG. Solflare is also a solid alternative.
What is the total supply, and can it change?
The total supply is fixed at 850,000,000 MOGG. Because the mint authority has been permanently revoked, this number cannot increase — ever. The supply is fully immutable and verifiable on-chain.
